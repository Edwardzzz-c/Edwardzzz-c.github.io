Cheng (Edward) Zhang

I am a computer science undergraduate at Columbia University. At Columbia, I am very fortunate to work with Dr. Zhanpeng He and Prof. Matei Ciocarlie at the Robotic Manipulation and Mobility (ROAM) Lab.

My research interests lie in robotics, machine learning, and computer vision. I currently focus on building algorithms and hardware for reliable robotic manipulation.
